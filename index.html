How it works

Three steps. No learning curve.

1

Record

Pick a monitor, a window, or your entire screen. Add your webcam and mic if you want. Press record. Done.

2

Review

Every click appears on a visual timeline. Adjust zoom levels, add text labels, trim the start and end. Or just leave it — the defaults are good.

3

Export

Hit export. Get a polished MP4 with smooth zoom animations, or an interactive HTML walkthrough your users can click through themselves.

Features

A proper recording studio.
Not just a gimmick.

ClickZoom is a full screen recorder with webcam, audio, timeline editing, and a trick up its sleeve.

🎯

Auto-Zoom to Every Click

The headline feature. Every mouse click gets a smooth, cubic-eased zoom animation. Configurable per-click — set zoom level from 1.5x to 4x, hold duration, and more. Your viewers never miss what you're showing them.

📸

Webcam Picture-in-Picture

Show your face alongside your screen with a live webcam overlay. Preview it in real-time before you start recording.

🎙️

Microphone Audio

Record voice narration alongside your screen. Captured as AAC and muxed into the final MP4 automatically.

🖥️

Multi-Monitor Support

Multiple screens? Pick the one you want. Or record a specific window. The source picker shows live thumbnails so you always know what you're getting.

✂️

Timeline Editor

Visual timeline with all your zoom points. Drag to reposition, delete the ones you don't want, add new ones manually. Trim start and end points. Full control, zero complexity.

🔗

Add to Existing Videos

Already have a recording? Import it. Append or prepend clips from different sessions. Different aspect ratios are letterboxed automatically. Build complete walkthroughs from pieces.

💬

Text Labels & Emoji Overlays

Add text callouts to any click point. Drop in emoji to draw attention or add personality. Your demos don't have to be boring.

🌐

Interactive HTML Export

Not just video — export step-by-step HTML walkthroughs. Your users click through at their own pace. Perfect for documentation and onboarding.

⏯️

Pause & Resume

Pause mid-recording. Grab coffee, collect your thoughts, rearrange your desktop. Resume when ready. No splicing required.

🖱️

Cursor & Click Ripples

Your cursor is drawn right onto the video frames. Each click gets a subtle ripple animation so viewers always know where to look.

🪶

Stupidly Simple UI

Clean, modern interface. No 47-button toolbar. No preferences maze. Record, edit, export — everything is exactly where you think it is.

💾

Crash-Safe Recording

Working files are saved as MKV which survives interruptions. Your recording is safe even if your cat walks on the keyboard.
