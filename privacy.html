Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

TL;DR: ClickZoom runs entirely on your computer. We don't collect your recordings, screen content, or personal data. The only data we receive is basic license verification.

What ClickZoom Does

ClickZoom is a screen recording application that runs locally on your Windows computer. All recordings, video processing, and editing happen on your device — nothing is uploaded to our servers.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the software:

  • License verification: When you activate a license, we verify it through Freemius (our licensing provider). This includes your license key and a machine identifier.
  • Update checks: The app periodically checks our server for new versions. This request includes only your app version number.
  • Feature requests: If you submit feedback through the app, we receive your message and optionally your email (if you provide it).

Data We Don't Collect

We do not collect, access, or store:

  • Your screen recordings or video content
  • Screenshots or images from your screen
  • Mouse clicks, keyboard input, or usage patterns
  • Personal files or documents
  • Webcam footage
  • Audio recordings

Third-Party Services

ClickZoom uses the following third-party services:

  • Freemius — License management and payment processing. See Freemius Privacy Policy.
  • Web3Forms — Feature request submissions. See Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All your recordings and project files are stored locally on your computer in locations you choose. We have no access to these files.

License information is stored locally in your user profile folder (~/.clickzoom/).

Children's Privacy

ClickZoom is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app or website.
